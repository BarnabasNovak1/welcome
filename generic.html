Actuary Course: Mathematical Finance and Interest Theory

This is an actuarial course approved by Society of Actuary in California. I am taking this at CSULA for fall of 2024. The course aligns with the syllabus for the Financial Mathematics (FM) exam and as such prepares students to take and pass that exam. This is a crash course in analitical risk management. I will learn how to evaluate the likelihood of events occuring, decrease the changes of undesirable events, and reducing the impact of undesireable events that do occur.
